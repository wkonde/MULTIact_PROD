POS-Point of sales Product Bundle Pack Odoo Apps

Point of sales Product Bundle Pack Odoo Apps module is used to offering several products for sale as one combined product in POS which you can able to sell as combo product kit easily from POS-Pint of sales Screen and able to deliver all products pack of kit in delivery orders.

POS receipt will generated based on product pack.You will maintain the stock or product pack as well as products in the products pack.
